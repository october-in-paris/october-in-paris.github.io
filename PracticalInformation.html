Venue

The workshop will be organised at Salle du Conseil de l'Observatoire de Paris (letter E on the map), 77, Avenue Denfert-Rochereau, 75014 (information available on the Paris Observatory website here).

IMPORTANT: The guards at the entrance of the Observatory will ask you for your name and an identity document to prove it. So don't forget to bring one with you!

How to get to the Paris Observatory

By metro or bus

Metro: station Denfert-Rochereau (lines 6 and 4)
RER B (express lines): station Denfert-Rochereau
Bus: 38, 68, 88

By train

From Gare du Nord: Take RER B heading South (to Denfert-Rochereau via Paris) or line 4 heading towards Porte d'Orléans, and get off at Denfert-Rochereau station. Travel Time: 30 mins.

From Gare de Lyon: Take line 14, heading towards Olympiades. Switch at Bercy station and take line 6, heading towards Charles de Gaulle-Étoile. Get off at Denfert-Rochereau. Travel time: 20 mins.

From Gare de l’Est: Take line 4 towards Mairie de Montrouge, and get off at Denfert-Rochereau station. Travel Time: 30 mins.

By plane

From Roissy Charles de Gaulle airport: Take RER B heading South (Denfert-Rochereau via Paris). Travel time: 1 hour. You can also take a shuttle Le Bus Direct Paris Aéroport Line 4 to Gare-Montparnasse, then line 4 towards Mairie de Montrouge, and get off at Denfert-Rochereau station. Travel time: 1 hour.

From Orly airport: Take the automatic metro Orlyval up to Antony station, then take RER B heading to Gare du Nord up to Denfert-Rochereau. Travel time: 40 mins.

Talks

For all speakers: please respect the time allocated to each talk (30 minutes in total, of which ~20-25 minutes for presentation and 5-10 minutes for questions). This will allow us to maximise exchanges, interactions and discussions.

It is our intention to make the pdf of your slides available to the participants after the workshop. If you do not wish to share your talk, please let us know before the end of the workshop.

Wifi & Zoom

The eduroam network will be available throughout the workshop. For those who do not have eduroam installed, it will still be possible to access the wifi (eduspot network) via a visitor account that will be provided with the badge at registration.

Lunches

For lunch, you can choose either to eat at the Observatory canteen, or enjoy the cafés and restaurants that are numerous in the area (on Rue Daguerre, for example). If you choose to eat at the Observatory canteen, the price of a meal is ~15 euros, except for the Observatory staff, who pay the usual rate.
